About INNOVIK 5.0

Welcome to INNOVIK Hackathon 5.0, where innovation meets technology! Join us for an extraordinary 36-hour coding marathon that brings together the brightest minds to solve real-world challenges through cutting-edge technology solutions.

This premier hackathon focuses on three critical themes: Healthcare Innovation, Sustainable Technology, and Cybersecurity Solutions. Participants will have the opportunity to work on 12 carefully curated problem statements that address pressing societal needs.

Problem Statements

01
AI-Powered Health Diagnostics
Software (AI/ML, Computer Vision)
Theme: Healthcare Innovation
Develop an AI-powered diagnostic tool that can analyze medical images (X-rays, CT scans, MRIs) to detect diseases like pneumonia, fractures, or tumors. The solution should provide accurate results with confidence scores and be accessible to healthcare professionals in remote areas with limited specialist availability.
02
Smart Medication Management
Hardware + Software (IoT, Mobile App)
Theme: Healthcare Innovation
Create a comprehensive medication management system that includes smart pill dispensers, mobile app reminders, dosage tracking, and integration with healthcare providers. The system should help elderly patients and those with chronic conditions maintain proper medication schedules and prevent adverse drug interactions.
03
Telemedicine Platform for Rural Areas
Software (Web/Mobile Platform)
Theme: Healthcare Innovation
Design a comprehensive telemedicine platform that works efficiently on low-bandwidth connections, supports multiple regional languages, includes basic diagnostic tools, and enables secure communication between patients in rural areas and healthcare professionals in urban centers.
04
Mental Health Support System
Software (AI Chatbot, Mobile App)
Theme: Healthcare Innovation
Develop an AI-powered mental health support platform that provides 24/7 emotional support, mood tracking, personalized therapy recommendations, crisis intervention, and connects users with professional counselors when needed. The system should maintain strict privacy and confidentiality standards.
05
Smart Energy Grid Management
Hardware + Software (IoT, AI, Blockchain)
Theme: Sustainable Technology
Create an intelligent energy grid management system that optimizes renewable energy distribution, predicts energy demand, manages load balancing, and enables peer-to-peer energy trading between households with solar panels or other renewable energy sources.
06
Waste Management Optimization
Hardware + Software (IoT, Route Optimization)
Theme: Sustainable Technology
Develop a smart waste management system that uses IoT sensors to monitor bin fill levels, optimizes collection routes, categorizes waste types, tracks recycling rates, and provides analytics to municipal authorities for better resource allocation and environmental impact reduction.
07
Carbon Footprint Tracker
Software (Mobile App, Data Analytics)
Theme: Sustainable Technology
Build a comprehensive carbon footprint tracking application that monitors individual and organizational carbon emissions from transportation, energy usage, food consumption, and lifestyle choices. Include gamification elements, reduction suggestions, and carbon offset marketplace integration.
08
Smart Agriculture System
Hardware + Software (IoT, AI, Drones)
Theme: Sustainable Technology
Create an intelligent farming system that uses IoT sensors, drones, and AI to monitor soil conditions, weather patterns, crop health, and automate irrigation. The system should optimize water usage, predict harvest times, and provide actionable insights to farmers for sustainable agriculture practices.
09
Advanced Threat Detection System
Software (AI/ML, Network Security)
Theme: Cybersecurity Solutions
Develop an AI-powered cybersecurity system that can detect and prevent advanced persistent threats, zero-day attacks, and insider threats in real-time. The system should use machine learning to adapt to new attack patterns and provide automated incident response capabilities.
10
Secure Digital Payments
Software (FinTech, Blockchain, Mobile)
Theme: Cybersecurity Solutions
Build a secure digital payment platform with advanced fraud detection, multi-factor authentication, and blockchain-based transaction verification. The system should be user-friendly for rural populations while maintaining bank-level security standards and supporting offline transactions.
11
Student Cyber Safety Platform
Software (Web/Mobile, AI Monitoring)
Theme: Cybersecurity Solutions
Create a comprehensive cyber safety platform for students that includes cyberbullying detection, safe browsing tools, digital literacy education, parental controls, anonymous reporting systems, and AI-powered content filtering to protect young users from online threats.
12
Next-Gen Authentication System
Hardware + Software (Biometrics, Blockchain)
Theme: Cybersecurity Solutions
Design a revolutionary authentication system that combines multiple biometric factors, behavioral analysis, and blockchain-based identity verification. The system should be passwordless, highly secure, privacy-preserving, and accessible across various devices and platforms.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team size: Up to 4 members; at least 1 female member is compulsory..
  • Eligibility: Open to students, developers, and innovators across India.
  • Originality: All code and assets must be original or properly licensed. Strictly no plagiarism.
  • Rounds: Multiple coding rounds, mentor checkpoints, and evaluations.
  • Judging criteria: Innovation, impact, feasibility, user experience, and presentation.
  • Code of conduct: Respectful, inclusive collaboration is mandatory.
